About Us

Established in 1981, Matha Steels Pvt. Ltd. has been at the forefront of the steel industry, providing superior flat steel products from leading manufacturers like AMNS, Tata Steelium, Tata Astrum, JSW, Posco, and SAIL.

Our Products

Cold Rolled Close Annealed (CRCA) Sheets

Thickness: 0.3mm to 3mm, Width: 900mm to 1500mm. Used in precision engineering, automotive body panels, and electrical enclosures.

Hot Rolled (HR) Sheets & Coils

Thickness: 1.6mm to 20mm, Width: 1250mm to 1500mm. Ideal for structural applications, heavy machinery, and industrial equipment.

Galvanized Plain (GP) & Galvanized Corrugated (GC) Sheets

Thickness: 0.3mm to 3mm, Width: 900mm to 1500mm. Used in roofing, fencing, and automotive parts.
